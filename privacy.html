Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2025

TransKit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the TransKit Chrome extension handles your information and why privacy is our top priority.

1. Data Collection and Usage

TransKit does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
  • Offline-First: The extension operates entirely offline using Chrome's built-in AI capabilities (Gemini Nano) by default.
  • No Remote Servers: When using the default Chrome AI provider, no data is sent to external servers. All processing occurs locally on your device.
  • No Tracking: The extension does not use analytics, trackers, cookies, or third-party scripts.
  • No User Accounts: We don't require or maintain any user accounts, logins, or profiles.

2. Optional AI Providers

TransKit allows you to optionally configure external AI providers (Google Gemini, OpenAI, DeepL, or OpenRouter). This is entirely optional and user-controlled.

  • API Keys: If you choose to use external providers, you must provide your own API keys. These keys are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and are never transmitted to us.
  • Third-Party Privacy: When using external providers, your translation requests are sent directly to those services. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies:
    • Google Gemini Privacy Policy
    • OpenAI Privacy Policy
    • DeepL Privacy Policy
    • OpenRouter Privacy Policy
  • Your Choice: You can use the default offline Chrome AI provider without ever connecting to any external service.

3. Permissions Explained

TransKit requests only the minimum permissions required for its core functionality:

  • Storage: Used solely to store local preferences (such as target language, enabled domains, and optional API keys) on your device. This data never leaves your device.
  • Offscreen: Required to access Chrome's built-in AI translation APIs (Gemini Nano) that need a DOM execution context.
  • Host Permissions (<all_urls>): Necessary to allow text selection and translation on any website you visit. The extension only activates when you explicitly trigger a translation.
These permissions are standard for translation extensions and allow TransKit to function without compromising your privacy.

4. Data Storage

All settings and preferences are stored locally using chrome.storage.local. This includes:

  • Your preferred target language
  • Enabled domains for instant translation
  • Custom language aliases
  • Optional API keys (if you configure external providers)

This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed when you uninstall the extension. We have no access to this information.

5. Open Source Transparency

TransKit is 100% open source. You can review the entire codebase at:
https://github.com/fernnguyen/transkit

This transparency allows security researchers, developers, and users to verify our privacy claims and ensure there are no hidden tracking mechanisms.

6. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your local settings at all times.

  • Access: All your settings are stored locally on your device
  • Modification: You can change settings anytime through the extension popup
  • Deletion: Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
  • Portability: Your settings are isolated to your device and browser profile

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of TransKit after any modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions about this Privacy Policy or TransKit's privacy practices, please contact us:

  • GitHub: Open an issue
  • Discussions: Join the discussion

Summary

TransKit is designed with privacy at its core. We don't collect, store, or transmit your personal data. All translation happens locally by default, and you have complete control over your settings. Our open-source nature ensures transparency and accountability.
